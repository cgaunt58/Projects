    History

    The Braves moved to Atlanta in 1966. After many years of struggling the team finally began to find success in early 90's. through the following decade the Braves became one of the most successfull teams in the league. They began a string of 15 straight division titles. Even though the experienced so much regular season success the Braves only managed to win a single World Series in the 90's. They would not celebrate another World Series title until 2021.
